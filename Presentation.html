So, what's next?

  • Content-strategy

    Guide your users through your product, don't just give them a half-hundred options and expect them to be able to sort it out on their own.

  • Internal links

    Stop thinking of end-points as end-points. Provide a method for your users to get around after they've arrived somewhere.

  • Contextual links

    Provide additional options to your users, 'Pages like this' and 'Did you mean?' suggestions are a good place to start.
